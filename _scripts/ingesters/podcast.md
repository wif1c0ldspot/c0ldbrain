# Podcast/Video Ingester

## Transcript Handling (MANDATORY noise stripping)

Remove: filler words ("um", "yeah"), meta-commentary, ad reads, sponsor segments, off-topic banter.
Keep: technical claims, stories illustrating concepts, disagreements, actionable advice.

## Extraction

- **Interview:** guest thesis, claims (distinguish opinions vs facts), tools/references
- **Conference talk:** findings, announcements, architecture details, Q&A highlights
- Transcripts can be 10K+ words — extract insights, don't summarize linearly
- Conference talks from major events = HIGH-SIGNAL
- Note publication date
