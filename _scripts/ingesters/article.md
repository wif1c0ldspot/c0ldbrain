# Article Ingester

## Extraction

- **Blog post:** thesis, novel patterns, tools mentioned, trade-offs, author bias
- **Tutorial:** what it teaches, prerequisites, key steps (condensed), gotchas
- **News:** WHAT happened, WHY it matters (skip hype/PR)

## Rules

- Most blogs: 20% signal, 80% storytelling — extract signal
- If duplicates existing wiki info → add citation to existing page, not duplicate
- Flag stale content with `> ⚠ STALE: from [date]`
