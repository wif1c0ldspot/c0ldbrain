How far you will go into God depends on your impression of Him
- Jesus is the material revelation of who God is

Our perception of God is unintentionally attached to our earthly father
- God is not a distant father
- He is a constant strength and encouragement
- When the devil distorts our idea of fathers, it distorts our identity 

### 6 Areas of which we develop misconceptions concerning God
1. Parental Authority
2. Parental Trustworthiness
	1. Our distrust in God hurts him very deeply
3. Parental Generosity
4. Parental affection 
	1. God rejects the mud, dirt (sin) but not the child
	2. When He reveals our sins and corrects us, it’s because of His love for us
	3. God feels our hurts more deeply than we do (psalms 56:8)
5. Parental attentiveness
	1. Gods ability to be with you all the time
	2. Unlike our earthly parents, no matter how perfect they may be they still fall short because we are finite beings 
6. Parental acceptance
	1. Many times in society acceptance is conditional
	2. However Gods love and acceptance is unconditional
	3. His love towards us is unconditional but His promises to us are conditiona
	4. Orphan mindset: living as though we are not children of God. Seeing God as a distant figure
		1. Behind every orphan mentality there is a spiritual stronghold
	5. His love is not something we achieve but something we receive
