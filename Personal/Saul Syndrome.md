## 9 Characteristics of Saul Syndrome
1. Withdrawal or isolation
	1. Cut off from others
	2. Issues in resolving problems with people
	3. Visible response: Difficult to reach, Inability to receive love, isolated, depressed
	4. Rejecting oneself before other can even reject you
2. Possessiveness
	1. Me first attitude
	2. Strives to meet own needs
	3. Manifestation of orphan mentality
	4. Constantly Comparing to people around you
	5. Crave recognition and validation
	6. Defensive when challenged
3. Us vs them mentality
	1. Behavioural grouping with people of the same attitude
	2. Defensive, wrong group of friends, create factions within the church
4. Judgmental and manipulative
	1. Demand things to be done their way
	2. Spiritualise reasons to justify their actions
	3. Tend to be proud
	4. Pride manifests in the form of control
5. Unteachable
	1. Closed to any form of correction or instruction 
	2. Quick to defend
	3. Often feels misunderstood
	4. Proverbs 12:1
6. Impatience
	1. Think that their way is the best way, and a refusal to listen to or take advice from others
	2. Frustration when things don’t align
	3. Don’t trust in Gods timing
	4. When we don’t trust in Him and His timing 
7. Distrust
	1. Often suspecting of people’s ulterior motives, finding it hard to trust others
8. Disloyalty
	1. A deep focus on one self in order to protect oneself from outside hurts and wounds
	2. Loyalty is conditional
	3. Serve their personal needs
9. Ingratitude
	1. Focus on simply what can be done for them instead of what has been done for them
