	1. During one of the youth camps in secondary school, I felt that God told me to end my relationship with my then partner. So when I heard from God I consulted my leader and had a chat with him. I can’t remember the details anymore, but I then proceeded to let her know what happened straight after.
	2. Another point of hurt in my life was when some session (maybe alpha course) the leader asked if anyone wanted to receive the gift of tongues. I said yes and tried to speak in tongues. I felt it was very fake and felt like I didn’t receive it because it was so unnatural. I remember feeling very hurt and disappointed and wondered why I didn’t receive it
	3. Also found out one of my ex was speaking ill of me behind my back with one of her good girlfriends. I can’t remember the exact contents but it was very shocking and hurtful to me because we were good and I didn’t expect it. I think when I asked why she would do that she also didn’t have any explanation and she just kept apologising. 
	4. Another time my 3rd ex secretly met up with her ex boyfriend below her house and intentionally hid it from me
	5. Once I was supposed to meet another ex but in the end she cancelled on me because she ended up in her exes house and didn’t meet me. She confessed that she slept with him instead of meeting me which was very hurtful at that point of time because of the thoughts that went in my head
	6. Another time when I was in Australia studying and we were already dating, I remember being hurt again because she confessed that she cried about her ex because she missed him when she was in Bangkok with her cousin because she travelled with him to Bangkok in the past. I also found out she had been secretly insta dming him as well. That was the first time that I experienced anxiety attacks 
	7. At the start of our relationship, there were many questions related to my past and many questions about me as a person and my character. My in laws weren't very accepting of me initially which was surprising to me because when we were just friends everything was good. I remember feeling like I was in a deep hole with no way out and exasperated from time to time. - How did that experience make you feel about yourself?
