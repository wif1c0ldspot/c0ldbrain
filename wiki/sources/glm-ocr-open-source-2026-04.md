## Summary

GLM-OCR, previously ranked #1 on OCR benchmarks, has been open sourced making state-of-the-art document recognition free.

- Usage limits

### Open Source (Old)

## GLM-OCR

### Capabilities
1. **High accuracy** — #1 on benchmarks
2. **Multiple formats** — PDF, image, scanned
3. **Handwriting** — Even messy notes
4. **Layout** — Tables, forms, mixed
5. **Languages** — Multi-language support

### Technical Details
- Transformer-based
- End-to-end training
- Custom preprocessing
- Post-processing pipeline
