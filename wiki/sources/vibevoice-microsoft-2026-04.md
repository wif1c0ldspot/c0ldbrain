# VibeVoice — Microsoft Voice AI Framework

## Key Insights

- Microsoft's comprehensive open-source voice AI framework
- Three specialized models: ASR-7B (speech recognition), TTS-1.5B (text-to-speech), Realtime-0.5B (real-time voice)
- Accepted as ICLR 2026 Oral — top-tier academic validation
- 39.6k stars: massive adoption for voice AI infrastructure

## Technical Details

### Model Suite
- **ASR-7B**: 7B parameter automatic speech recognition — high accuracy transcription
- **TTS-1.5B**: 1.5B parameter text-to-speech — natural voice synthesis
- **Realtime-0.5B**: 0.5B parameter real-time voice model — low-latency conversation

### Architecture
- Unified framework supporting all three voice AI tasks
- Modular design — use individual models or full pipeline
- Optimized for both cloud and edge deployment
- Multilingual support

### ICLR 2026 Oral
- Top-tier conference acceptance as Oral presentation
- Indicates groundbreaking research contribution
- Peer-reviewed validation of approach

### Capabilities
- Real-time conversational voice AI
- Accurate transcription across accents and noise
- Natural-sounding speech synthesis
- Voice cloning and adaptation
- Streaming inference for live applications
