# Teknium: Hermes LLM-Wiki Integration

**Source:** https://x.com/teknium/status/2041370915012071577

## Summary

Teknium (Hermes creator) announced that Hermes Agent now ships with Karpathy's LLM-Wiki built-in. The workflow is: `hermes update` then `/llm-wiki <research x>`. This validates our approach and shows Nous Research is building knowledge base capabilities as a core feature.

## Key Facts
- Official support for LLM-Wiki in Hermes Agent
- Command: `/llm-wiki <research x>`
- Integrates with Obsidian vaults
- Demonstrated with real research corpus from Nous projects
